## About Me

I build products, investigate digital threats, and research security vulnerabilities across the full stack and the full lifecycle.

My work spans three areas that directly reinforce each other. As a Full Stack Developer, I take products from zero to production. I co-built KlickSync, a studio management and billing platform now live with paying clients. As an OSINT Researcher, I built EthicOSINT, an investigation platform purpose-built for law enforcement agencies, police departments, and cyber cells. As a Security Engineer, I have responsibly disclosed critical vulnerabilities at NASA, Citibank, Lenovo, and more, earning Hall of Fame recognition at each.

Before that, I founded HacksterGPT, one of the first AI chatbots purpose-built for the cybersecurity and ethical hacking community, grew it organically, and successfully sold it in September 2024.

As CTO at Cydenium, I lead cybersecurity training and infrastructure. As Co-Founder of CyberEthic, I oversee the development of security tooling, OSINT platforms, labs, CTFs, and threat intelligence research built for real-world investigative work.

The approach across everything is the same: understand how systems work, find where they fail, and build something better.
